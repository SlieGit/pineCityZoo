ANIMALS MAP PLACES

TIGER

the largest cat

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest cat species, most recognizable for its pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside. It is an apex predator, primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and bovids.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the tigers in the Tiger Enclosure, which is located near the entrance of the zoo.
